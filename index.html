The American School of Bombay follows a college preparatory program that culminates with an American high school diploma (ASB Diploma) or the International Baccalaureate Diploma (IB Diploma). ASB is a fully recognized IB World School utilizing an international curriculum, having received full accreditation from the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools in the US. It is also accredited by the Commission on International and Trans-Regional Accreditation in the US. The International Baccalaureate program was established at ASB in 1998. ASB's International Baccalaureate Primary Years (IB PYP) and International Baccalaureate Diploma (IB DP) Programs are authorized by the International Baccalaureate Organization (IBO) in Geneva, Switzerland.
